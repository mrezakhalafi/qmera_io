How To Close Sales When Customers Are Ready To Buy

SHARE

According to Think with Google, more than half of shoppers do research first before buying a product to ensure they make “the best possible choice.” When they come to your online store, they likely already know what they are looking for. So it is not how you convince them, but how you engage them that will help decide whether they buy from your shop or look elsewhere.

So how can you successfully close sales when customers have already done their research and are ready to buy?

Make it easy.

Customers shop online primarily for convenience. According to Shopify, customers abandon their carts when they experience inconveniences — including payment issues, technical issues, and lack of product information. To prevent customers from buying outside your shop, make it easy for them to get all the necessary details about the product and complete the transaction.

Let’s take a look at some ways e-marketers like you can make it super easy for buyers to buy products online.

  • For hospitality. Keep all information, communications, and calls within a single app so that customers will be encouraged to arrange bookings inside the booking system. Engage with them in real-time through in-app messaging. Enable them to quickly and effortlessly find hotels, destinations, and experiences through a smart search bot. Help them make decisions fast — and without having to do expensive and time-consuming property ocular visits — through virtual tours, live streaming, or static videos.

  • For financial institutions. Let customers open an account from their mobile phones. Answer their questions quickly using smart bots. Allow customers to easily start video calls and show tellers the required documents without having to do it physically or outside the company’s mobile app.

  • For retailers. Do live selling so customers can interact with sellers in real-time and buy products right there and then. Enable customers to easily find the product they need using a smart search bot. Let them effortlessly inquire about the product through video call, VoIP, and/or chat.

  • For healthcare. Enable real-time consultation and live coaching through live streaming. Make the experience even better by allowing viewers to interact with the streamer through chat features available during the live stream. Use smart bots to make it easy for customers to get product information and find the product they need.

Be personal. Be conversational.

Conversational engagement is crucial for closing sales. Today’s customers want personalized experiences. By providing them with a mobile platform that enables them to instantly start a conversation, quickly get an appropriate response, easily check out an item, and make payments, customers are encouraged to buy from your shop.

Ensure everything is at their fingertips.

To provide customers greater convenience, allow them to complete the transaction without using multiple platforms and/or visiting multiple sites. Roll out your store’s app (if you haven’t done it yet) and supercharge it with conversational engagement capabilities to successfully and immediately close sales.
